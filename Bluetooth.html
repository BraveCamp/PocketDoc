Bluetooth

Bluetooth Description

You can use bluetooth to connect your device to this website so that you can track your symptoms and health from any device of your choice.

Warning

If you turn off the privacy in your account settings, others might be able to connect to your account through their device and view your information. MAKE SURE YOUR ACCOUNT IS PRIVATE BEFORE TURNING ON THE BLUETOOTH OPTION (in the settings).


Connect To A Device


Once you're connected, you can start using your device to track your health. If you need any help setting up your device CLICK HERE for Apple or CLICK HERE for Android to watch a brief video explaining how to connect your device to bluetooth.
